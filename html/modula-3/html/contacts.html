Keeping in touch

Comp.lang.modula3

comp.lang.modula3 is a Usenet newsgroup devoted to Modula-3. There you will find discussions on the language and how to use it, annoucements of releases (both of SRC Modula-3 and of other systems).

If your local NNTP server has comp.lang.modula3, clicking here will get you there.

The archives of comp.lang.modula3 are available via anonymous ftp from gatekeeper.dec.com and its mirrors in pub/DEC/Modula-3/comp.lang.modula3.

There is also a kwic (Key Word In Context) index to help find the messages on a given subject.

Mailing list

Since not everybody has access to Usenet, we also maintain a relay mailing list, to which we resend the articles appearing in comp.lang.modula3. Every few hours, the new messages sent to comp.lang.modula3 are forwarded to the members of the list.

To be added to this list, send a message to m3-request@src.dec.com.

Messages sent to m3@src.dec.com are automatically forwarded to comp.lang.modula3

Reporting bugs

We prefer that you send bug reports to m3-request@src.dec.com. After we have reviewed your report, we may post an article in comp.lang.modula3, describing the bug and a workaround or a fix.

Needless to say, this implementation probably has many bugs. We are quite happy to receive bug reports. We can't promise to fix them, but we will try. When reporting a bug, please send us a short program that demonstrates the problem.
